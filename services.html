HR consulting covers areas such as talent acquisition, performance management, learning and development, compensation nd benefits, employee relations, compliance with labour laws, and organisational development. consultants assess existing HR systems, identify gaps, and recommend best practices to enhance efficiency and employee engagement. by offering an external and objective perspective, HR consultants help organisations manage change, implement new HR strategies, and adopt modern HR technologies.
